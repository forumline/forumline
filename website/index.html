what is forumline ??

forumline is a community platform that combines the best parts of old-school forums with modern features. threaded discussions that are actually searchable, real-time chat for when you want to hang out, and voice rooms for talking to your people.

~ how it works ~

every forum is its own independent instance. you own your data, you control your community. but through federation, your members can use one identity across all forumline forums. no more making a new account everywhere you go.

it's what forums should have evolved into, instead of everything becoming discord servers where knowledge goes to die in an infinite scroll.
